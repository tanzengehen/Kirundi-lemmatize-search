The Lemmatiser distinguishes between the following PoS:


ADJ - adjectives
ADV - adverbs
CONJ - Conjunctions
EMAIL - Email-addresses (anonymised)
F - foreign words
INTJ - Interjections
NOUN - nouns
NUM - numbers
NUM_ROM - roman numbers
PRON - pronouns
PROPN - proper names
PROPN_CUR - names of currencies
PROPN_LOC - geographical names
PROPN_NAM - personal names
PROPN_ORG - names of organisations
PROPN_PER - names for groups of persons
PROPN_REL - religious names
PROPN_SCI - scientific names
PROPN_THG - names of products
PROPN_VEG - locale names of plants
PRP - prepositions
SYMBOL - symbols
UNK - uncategorised tokens
VERB - verbs
WWW - webaddresses (anonymised)
